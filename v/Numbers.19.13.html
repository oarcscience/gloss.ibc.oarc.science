BSB Numbers 19:13. Debug: verse number 4303Anyone who touches a human corpse and fails to purify himself defiles the tabernacle of the lord. That person must be cut off from Israel. He remains unclean, because the water of purification has not been sprinkled on him, and his uncleanness is still on him.
