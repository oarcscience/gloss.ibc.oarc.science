BSB Ezekiel 46:12. Debug: verse number 14029When the prince makes a freewill offering to the lord, whether a burnt offering or a peace offering, the gate facing east must be opened for him. He is to offer his burnt offering or peace offering just as he does on the Sabbath day. Then he shall go out, and the gate must be closed after he goes out.
